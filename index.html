Welcome to Economic Model of Retinopathy of Prematurity EcROP

Retinopathy of Prematurity (ROP) is a growing epidemic found in many places around the world. The Introduction of the Economic Model is to show the Monitary and Societal benefits of the treatment of ROP. This application seeks to arm ophthmalogist with a cost-to-benefit anaylsis of the treatment of ROP. If you want to learn more about ROP or the importance of the Econmic Model please continue to the research tab found on the top navigation bar, it will lead you many resources on the topic as well as contact information to some of the researchers leading the initiative.


Below you find access to the Calculator. The Calculator helps analysis the cost-benefit trade of ROP by taking large batch of inputs collected from the regional being assessed. The inputs show the calculator all the variables that make the cots anaylsis within the region unique.
